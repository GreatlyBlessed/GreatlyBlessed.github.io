About Us

Greatly Bless is a group that loves to sing Southern Gospel Music and we've been singing together for over 8 years. Our desire is to sing the music that we love, for the One that we love and serve. Greatly Blessed is a group of individuals that possess the sound that has been produced by countless hours of hard work and an abundance of dedication.

We are all active members at the WIndsor Church of God (Windsor, PA). We are made up of Musicians, Sunday School Teachers, Sunday School and Church Officers.

Linda Billet is retired after many years at Quakers Oats. She is a Church Council member and can play the organ.

Vada Himes does volunteer work at the York Hospital after years as a Nurse as well as a Dallastown School Nurse. Vada also sometimes plays piano for us. Vada is also a Church Council Member.

Bonnie Neff (wife of Dale) worked for years at a printing company, and now works part time at Sam's Club (York) and at Better Hearing (Dallastown & York). She is the Kindergarten Teacher at Windsor Church of God. Bonnie and Dale will be married for 51 years in August 2019.

Roland Fauth was and is a beautician for many years. He plays piano and organ, and whatever else he attempts. He is also a Church Council Member. (Roland is married to Linda who is our Number one supporter/helper, projectionist, etc) In August, they will be celebrating 44 years of marriage.

Dale Neff is the manager of the group and selects most of the music that we use. He also coaches baseball (Seventeen years as assistant at York Township. Currently at Jacobus, and recently retired from Penn State York Baseball after 10 years). Dale is also a Church Council Member and also a Congregational Music Director.

On September 28, 2019, Vada, Bonnie, and Dale will celebrate singing together for 50 years. They started out singing in a group called "The Gospel 3".
